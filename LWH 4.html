About Me

I am a blessed child. Like anyone searching for happiness and direction, I have ups and downs. Over the past 30 years, I have had many opportunities to discover new people, places and a new me. For that, I am truly thankful.

At every stage, I strive to give my best. Never fully satisfied with the results but know that growth is a journey. Over the years, I have learned to manage my weakness, develop my strength, and uncover hidden talents I never knew I had.

Looking at my life path, you will see that I have varied career paths in different fields. Some may read it as lacking a clear direction. Others may interpret it as multi-talented. The truth is, it reflects my honesty, with myself and with the times.
